Go Keto!

The reasons why this could be the best thing for you,

A keto diet refers to a ketogenic diet, which is a high-fat, adequate-protein, low-carb diet. The goal is to get more calories from protein and fat than from carbs. It works by depleting your body of its store of sugar, so it will start to break down protein and fat for energy, causing ketosis (and weight loss).

One popular recipe below, Keto Pizza!

Ingredients

Instructions

Crust
4 eggs
6 oz. shredded cheese, preferably mozzarella or provolone

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. Start by making the crust. Crack eggs into a medium-sized bowl and add shredded cheese. Give it a good stir to combine.

3. Use a spatula to spread the cheese and egg batter on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. You can form two round circles or just make one large rectangular pizza.

Topping
3 tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp dried oregano
5 oz. shredded cheese
1½ oz. pepperoni
olives (optional)

 

4. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes until the pizza crust turns golden. Remove and let cool for a minute or two.

5. Increase the oven temperature to 450°F (225°C).

6. Spread tomato paste on the crust and sprinkle oregano on top. Top with cheese and place the pepperoni and olives on top.

 

For serving
5 oz. leafy greens
4 tbsp olive oil
sea salt and ground black pepper

7. Bake for another 5-10 minutes or until the pizza has turned a golden brown color.

8. Serve with a fresh salad on the side.
